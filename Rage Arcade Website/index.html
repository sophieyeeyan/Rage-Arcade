An app for you to relieve stress

Rage Arcade is a local game for urban dwellers in Hong Kong to relieve stress, addressing how stressful lives can be living in the city. The game booths are placed at different corners of the cities.

Local Game

You can find this game in different corners of the city. In train station, in the streets and inside mall. You can play it with your friends or even strangers. With more people, you can finish the level and achieve high score together!

Lets Scream!

We challenge you to scream as loud as you can and as long as you can. Within one minute, you goal is to destroy the item with your loudest voice. If you are tired, invite others to join you. The app is currently under development of other levels
